What is EMI?

EMI (Equated Monthly Installment) is the fixed amount you pay every month towards your loan, including both principal and interest.
Loan Amount: The original amount of money borrowed.
Interest Rate: The percentage charged on the principal amount, which can be Fixed (constant) or Floating (varies with market rates).
Tenure: The total duration within which the loan must be repaid.
Total Amount: The sum of the principal and interest paid over the entire loan tenure.
